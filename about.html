I began filmmaking in 2012, originally creating shorts with a handful of friends who were crazy enough to stick with me through the process. I've since worked on several official sets producing narrative, documentary, and commercial media, serving a wide variety of crew positions. "Illogical Productions" are my personal projects — stories that I can't help but tell, that push my cinematic skills, and that help me make sense of… well, everything. I just hope they can help someone else figure things out too.
