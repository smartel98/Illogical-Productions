I started making films in 2012, originally creating shorts with a handful of friends who were crazy enough to stick with me through the process. I've since worked on numerous sets producing narrative, documentary, and commercial media, serving on a wide variety of crew positions.

Illogical Productions is the space for my personal projects — because the world is illogical, life doesn't make sense. These are stories I have to tell, to push my cinematic skills and help me understand… well, something. Who knows, maybe they'll help someone else figure things out too.
